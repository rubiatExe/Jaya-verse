# **App Name**: Jaya's Universe

## Core Features:

- Animated Landing Page: Animated Landing Page: A beautiful, welcoming title ("A Little Universe for Jaya") with a shimmering animation.
- Public Life RPG View: Life RPG (View-Only): Visitors can see Jaya's current status (like "studying" or "napping") but cannot change it.
- Recent Love Feed: Recent Love Feed: A live-updating feed that shows the last few messages and reasons left by friends and family.
- Public Water Status: View Jaya's daily water intake progress.
- Leave a Public Note: Leave a personal note for one of the "Open When..." letter categories.
- Share a Reason (Public): Share a reason they love Jaya.
- Add Pin to Public Map: Add their location to the interactive friend map.
- Interactive Life RPG: Interactive Life RPG: Jaya can update her status, which is then saved and shown to everyone on the public page. She can also add specific details about her activity (e.g., what book she's reading).
- Friend Map: Friend Map: A visual world map displaying pins from all her friends. She can hover over each pin to see who it is and the message they left. It also includes a "Global Friends" widget showing the current local time for each friend.
- Reasons We Love You Jar: Shared "Reasons We Love You" Jar: An AI tool that displays a random reason she is loved, pulling from a list you wrote and all the submissions from her friends.
- Interactive Open When Letters: Interactive "Open When..." Letters: She can open letters for different moods, filled with messages submitted by her loved ones. She has the ability to see a new note or delete ones she doesn't want to keep.
- Water Tracker: Water Tracker: A personal tracker to log her daily water intake, which grows a digital plant that blooms when she reaches her goal.
- Self-Talk Sesh: Self-Talk Sesh: An empowering exercise that challenges self-doubt with positive reinforcement.
- Cycle Tracker: Cycle Tracker: A simple, private tracker for her menstrual cycle.
- Dream Board: Dream Board: A visual, collage-style board where she can add and manage her personal goals and dreams.
- Background Music: Soothing piano music plays in the background.
- Heart Animation: Clicking anywhere on the screen creates a smooth, heart-beating animation.
- Dog and Butterfly: An animated dog chases a butterfly along the bottom of the screen, with its behavior changing based on her Life RPG status.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Light pastel pink (#F9A8D4) to evoke feelings of love, warmth, and gentleness.
- Background color: Very light pastel pink (#FDE2F3), almost white, for a soft and comforting feel.
- Accent color: Pale lavender (#E1BEE7) for interactive elements and highlights, adding a touch of magic and whimsy.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif, to give a clean modern readable feel to most text.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', serif, for headers.
- Use soft, rounded icons with a hand-drawn feel to convey friendliness and approachability.
- Incorporate gentle animations for transitions and feedback, such as scaling up elements on hover, or subtle fades and slides to create a smooth user experience.
